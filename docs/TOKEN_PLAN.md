# ShieldMendAi Token Information

## Status

SMEND is the ShieldMendAi community support token on Base, launched through
Bankr. ShieldMendAi remains a product-first, pre-alpha software project.

The token does not represent ownership, guaranteed product access, promised
utility, a claim on revenue, or a promise of future value.

## Intended role

SMEND is positioned as a community support mechanism around the public
development effort. Potential support areas include development expenses,
infrastructure, AI service costs, testing, security improvements,
documentation, and community operations.

No future utility, value, market, funding result, or development outcome is
guaranteed.

## Product capability boundary

Current ShieldMendAi execution is deterministic simulation only. Live
monitoring, service restarts, production repairs, real notification delivery,
customer deployment, and autonomous production recovery are unavailable.

See the authoritative [Builder Roadmap](roadmap.html) and
[Project Status](../PROJECT_STATUS.md).
