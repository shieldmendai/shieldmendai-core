Why Robinhood Chain?

A simple EVM-compatible network for onchain finance.

Robinhood Chain is an EVM-compatible network built for onchain finance. It uses ETH for gas and is an open network for builders.

EVM-compatible

Designed to work with familiar Ethereum-style tooling and contract standards.

Uses ETH for gas

Network activity is paid for with ETH on Robinhood Chain.

Built for onchain finance

The chain is positioned around finance use cases and onchain access.

Open to builders

Developers can build public network experiences and ecosystem tools.

Important

Community token only.

The community token is being created as a community and ecosystem token around ShieldMendAI. It does not represent ownership, equity, revenue rights, or a claim on ShieldMendAI assets or operations.

This is a community token. It is not equity. It does not grant a claim on company revenue, profits, assets, app fees, or holdings. It is not required to use the ShieldMendAI app unless future utility is clearly announced.

No promises of price, profit, returns, staking yield, ownership, revenue share, or investment performance are being made.
