The problem is simple: regular builders need smarter protection.

Builders on Base are launching bots, automations, tools, websites, and experiments every day. But most small builders do not have a full DevOps team watching their servers.

When a service crashes, a bot stalls, a process dies, or alerts are missed, real projects can lose momentum fast.

ShieldMendAI is being built to help detect failures, recover services, alert owners, and create readable recovery logs before small problems become disasters.

What ShieldMendAI v0.1 Will Deliver

The first public release will focus on a simple, reusable, builder-friendly self-healing core.

🛡️ Monitor

Watch critical services, bot processes, and server health signals.

⚡ Recover

Restart failed services and trigger safe recovery workflows when something breaks.

📲 Alert

Send simple Telegram alerts so builders know what happened without living in a terminal.

📘 Log

Create readable incident logs that explain failures, actions taken, and next steps.

🔐 Separate

Extract the public core safely away from private bot code, keys, wallets, and configs.

🚀 Launch

Publish a clean v0.1 that other builders can test, inspect, and improve.

Milestone Plan
