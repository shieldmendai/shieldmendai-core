Security

Planning software, not custody software.

ShieldMendAI is designed around public wallet analysis and exportable records. You stay in control of your assets and transaction decisions.

Never seed phrases

ShieldMendAI never asks for seed phrases or private keys.

Never custody

ShieldMendAI never takes custody and never requests trading permissions.

ShieldMendAI provides estimated tax-planning information only. It is not tax, legal, accounting, or financial advice. Confirm with a qualified tax professional before filing or making major financial decisions.
