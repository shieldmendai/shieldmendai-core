FAQ

Common questions.

Does ShieldMendAI trade for me?

No. ShieldMendAI is read-only planning software and does not request trading permissions.

Is this tax advice?

No. Outputs are estimated tax-planning information only. Confirm with a qualified tax professional.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Users can export raw CSV files, and paid plans or Tax Packs include broader reporting outputs.

What happens if I cancel?

Archive Mode keeps saved wallets and raw CSV downloads available, while paid scan and reporting features pause.

ShieldMendAI provides estimated tax-planning information only. It is not tax, legal, accounting, or financial advice. Confirm with a qualified tax professional before filing or making major financial decisions.
