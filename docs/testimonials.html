Why ShieldMendAI Exists

Servers should not just fail silently. They should fight to recover.

ShieldMendAI started from a real problem: builders lose too much time fixing servers, restarting broken services, checking logs, and trying to figure out what went wrong after something already failed.

The mission is to build a self-healing shield for servers — a system that can monitor services, detect failures, recover automatically, alert the owner, and learn from previous incidents over time.

The long-term goal is simple: make server recovery easier for regular people, small teams, builders, and operators who need reliable infrastructure without needing to live inside a terminal all day.
