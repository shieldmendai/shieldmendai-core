How It Works

Preview the tax impact before you move.

ShieldMendAI helps you compare possible outcomes before you sell. It does not trade, custody assets, or replace a qualified tax professional.

1. Add wallet

Paste a public wallet address for read-only analysis.

2. Review token lots

Choose a token and inspect estimated lots, basis, and gain or loss ranges.

3. Simulate sale

Enter the amount you are considering selling and review estimated tax impact.

4. Export records

Download CSV files and planning records for tax-prep conversations.

ShieldMendAI provides estimated tax-planning information only. It is not tax, legal, accounting, or financial advice. Confirm with a qualified tax professional before filing or making major financial decisions.
