How It Works

Understand your wallet before you move.

ShieldMendAI helps you review holdings, profit/loss, rewards, airdrops, tax lots, and possible sale outcomes before you sell. It does not trade, custody assets, or replace a qualified tax professional.

1. Add wallet

Paste a public wallet address for read-only analysis.

2. Review holdings

See tokens, estimated cost basis, profit/loss, rewards, airdrops, and newly received coins.

3. Compare sale options

Inspect lots, long-term versus short-term status, and estimated tax impact before making a sale elsewhere.

4. Export records

Download CSV files and proof-style planning records for tax-prep conversations.

ShieldMendAI provides estimated tax-planning information only. It is not tax, legal, accounting, or financial advice. Confirm with a qualified tax professional before filing or making major financial decisions.
