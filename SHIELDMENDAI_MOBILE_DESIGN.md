# ShieldMendAI Website And Mobile Design

## Direction

ShieldMendAI is light-first. The default website and mobile app direction is a white, premium fintech/SaaS interface with near-black text, purple accents, clean cards, and confident plain-English labels.

The product is a wallet-first crypto holdings, profit/loss, and tax-planning app for everyday holders. Staking rewards, airdrops, unlocks, and newly received coins are built-in wallet intelligence categories for users who have them.

Dark mode can be added later as an alternate mode. Dark sections may be used sparingly for contrast, trust messaging, or high-emphasis product moments, but the product should not feel like an all-dark crypto template.

## Light Palette

- Background: `#FFFFFF`
- Soft background: `#F7F5FF`
- Card: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#0B0B12`
- Muted text: `#5E5A6D`
- Purple primary: `#7C3AED`
- Purple bright: `#A855F7`
- Purple dark: `#4C1D95`
- Purple soft: `#EDE9FE`
- Border: `rgba(17, 16, 24, 0.10)`

## Dark Contrast Palette

- Background: `#05050A` or `#080812`
- Text: `#FFFFFF`
- Purple glow: accent only

## Style Principles

- Use white as the default background.
- Use black or near-black for primary text.
- Use purple gradients for primary actions and premium accents.
- Use green only for minor success or check indicators.
- Use clean rounded cards, bento grids, layered shadows, and product/dashboard mockups.
- Keep labels short and plain-English.
- Make the mobile app match the same light-first brand direction.
- Do not publish real invite strings, placeholder invite strings, or public release links.
- Lead with wallet holdings, profit/loss, cost basis, tax lots, and What If I Sell? planning.
- Present staking, rewards, airdrops, unlocks, and new received coins as built-in tracking categories.

## Inspiration Rules

The design may take high-level inspiration from premium fintech and SaaS landing pages, including clean spacing, strong headlines, polished dashboard mockups, bento cards, and trust/security sections.

Do not copy third-party source code, exact wording, protected layouts, images, icons, brand assets, or unique trade dress.

## Trust Guardrails

ShieldMendAI must stay read-only and non-custodial. Trust points should remain visible near important actions.

- Do not ask for seed phrases.
- Do not ask for private keys.
- Do not prompt for wallet approvals.
- Do not imply custody, swaps, trading, or transaction signing.
- Reinforce public wallet analysis, no custody, no approvals, and estimates-only tax planning.
- Keep beta access private. The public site may show the beta access form, but it must not show invite strings.
