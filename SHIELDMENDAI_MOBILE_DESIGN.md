# ShieldMendAI Mobile Design

## Theme Direction

ShieldMendAI should feel like a premium fintech product: dark, focused, readable, and calm. The mobile experience is dark-theme first, with light-theme support planned for a later pass.

## Dark Palette

- Background: `#05050A`
- Deep background: `#080812`
- Surface: `#10101A`
- Card: `#151522`
- Text: `#FFFFFF`
- Muted text: `#B8B8C7`
- Purple primary: `#8B5CF6`
- Purple bright: `#A855F7`
- Purple glow: `rgba(139, 92, 246, 0.35)`
- Border: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.10)`

## Future Light Palette

- Background: `#FFFFFF`
- Surface: `#F7F5FF`
- Card: `#FFFFFF`
- Text: `#111018`
- Muted text: `#5E5A6D`
- Purple primary: `#7C3AED`
- Purple soft: `#EDE9FE`
- Border: `rgba(17, 16, 24, 0.10)`

## UI Principles

- Use purple, white, and black as the brand foundation.
- Use green only for minor success or check indicators.
- Keep cards clean, flat, and easy to scan.
- Use large readable buttons with plain-English labels.
- Keep layouts mobile-first with minimal clutter.
- Make trust points visible near high-intent actions.

## Trust Guardrails

ShieldMendAI must stay read-only and non-custodial.

- Do not ask for seed phrases.
- Do not ask for private keys.
- Do not prompt for wallet approvals.
- Do not imply custody, swaps, trading, or transaction signing.
- Reinforce read-only wallet analysis, no custody, and no approvals in the product surface.
